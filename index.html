WHAT WE OFFER

Thrift Collection

We encourage thrift among members so that each member may cultivate the habit of making savings at least once monthly, with a view to building up funds for their individual future use.

Financial Advisory

We provide professional/Financial Advisory Services - Investment appraisal, feasibility studies, business plans etc.

Short Term Loans

We provide short-term loans for our members at a minimal interest rate with convenient terms of repayment.

Store Services

We offer a variety of store services to meet the needs of our members, including discounts and special offers.

Real Estate

We provide real estate services to help our members find the perfect property for their needs.

Business Services

We offer a range of business services to support our members in their entrepreneurial endeavors.
